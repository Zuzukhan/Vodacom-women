How to empower women in your community

Amplify women's voices, ideas and opinions.If a woman has a great idea, champion her and make sure she gets the recognition.

As many of the inspirational women from Vodacom have done, Mentor girls and women in an effort share knowledge and develop the skills of those around you. Reach out and influence the life of a girl in your own community
Offer women more job opportunities. Despite being significant contributors to social and financial development women do not have access to equal job opportunities. The solution to this is making remuneration equal amoungst all people.
Validate women's self-expression. When you see a woman taking it upon herself to share her story and let herself be vulnerable, acknowledge that act of bravery and offer solidarity and support.
Remember change does not happen overnight, but by implementing some of these tips in your everyday life, the community is taking a crucial step towards gender equality and women empowerment
